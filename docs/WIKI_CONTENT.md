## 🎯 Why heyai-ruleset?
Modern AI assistants are powerful, but they often lack the project-specific context needed to be truly effective. This ruleset ensures your AI:
1. Understands your project structure instantly.
2. Follows your coding standards without being asked.
3. Maintains a consistent identity across sessions.
